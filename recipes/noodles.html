Description

This is a recipe to make all types of noodles, from indomie to korean ramen it applies to all.

This method has been tried and tested and provides the best results. The procedure may vary from the convectional as well as from the back of your noodle packets. But all I can say is TRUST ME.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Soy sauce (optional)
  • Boiled eggs (optional)

Steps

  1. Place your noodles of choice in your sufuria and add water until fully covered
  2. Turn the heat on and wait for the water to boil for around a minute
  3. Taste a strand of the noodle to check on the texture, ensuring that it is not hard and undercooked
  4. As soon as your preferred texture is attained, lower the heat and drain 70% of the water. Do not dispose off of the noodle water
  5. To the remainder in the sufuria, add all the flavourings in as well as a dash of soy sauce and bring this to a mix
  6. Once fully mixed, begin to reintroduce the noodle water little by little while tasting, ensuring not to dilute the ramen
  7. When the soup remaining is to your liking, turn off the gas and serve together with the boiled eggs
