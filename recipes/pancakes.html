Description

Pancakes or crepes, the easiest way possible while tasting great

I think sometimes it is unnecesarry to go all out with food, I think pancakes should be vanilla flavoured only It's okay to be simple, it works best imo.

Ingredients

  • All Purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • 2 eggs
  • Vanilla essence
  • Cooking oil
  • Honey
  • Nuttella
  • Fruits

Steps

  1. Whist two eggs in a bowl, and add sugar a pinch of salt, vanilla essence and milk
  2. After fully mixing, add all purpose flour, till the correct texture is obtained.
  3. If the mixture is too solid, add milk or water, If too runny continue adding flour
  4. Afterwards add enough oil to the mixture
  5. Heat up a pan in medium heat and pour a serving spoon full of the mixture to the pan
  6. Once the bottom is cooked well, flip the pancake and cook on the other side
  7. Continue doing this till all the mixture is done
  8. Serve by drizzling nuttella or honey and topping up with fruits, preferrably when warm
