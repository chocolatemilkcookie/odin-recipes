Description

We are going to be making some yummy caramelized cheese toast, which is not only delicious but also super easy to make. It's the perfect snack and even kids can make it.

To make it even better only a few ingredients and tools are required. What are you waiting for, let's dive right in!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter or margarine
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Honey
  • Ham (optional)

Steps

  1. Butter two slices of bread on the outside
  2. Place one slice of the bread on the toaster with the buttered side facing down
  3. Slice or grate mozarella cheese, till it is enough to cover the bread on the toaster
  4. Add ham on top of the cheese (optional)
  5. Cover with the remaining slice and let this toast for 3 minutes
  6. Cover one side of the bread with honey
  7. Toast the bread again for 3 min or until the outside is crispy and caramelized
  8. Remove the bread from the toaster and enjoy while hot
